There's an art to the perfect olive oil-fried egg. Here's how to do it

Source: How to Make a Perfect Olive Oil-Fried Egg - Bon Appétit

I'm not a huge fried egg fan but this method works for me. Good olive oil, crack an egg into it, occasionally splash some hot oil on the yolk and let the edges get crispy. I add Boursin cheese and eat it with buttered toast.
