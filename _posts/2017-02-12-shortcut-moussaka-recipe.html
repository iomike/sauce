This recipe is by Melissa Clark and takes 2 hours. Tell us what you think of it at The New York Times - Dining - Food.

Source: Shortcut Moussaka Recipe - NYT Cooking

I made this and followed the recipe closely. I take offence at the title, it's not a "shortcut". I'll make this again but I would definitely make some changes.

Next time:

  • skip the breadcrumbs
  • use canned diced tomatoes instead of fresh. We found it was dryer than expected.
  • incorporate thinly sliced potatoes.

I'm not into "deconstructed" recipes but you could separate this easily into a potato side with the meat/eggplant/sliced potato/onion mix.
